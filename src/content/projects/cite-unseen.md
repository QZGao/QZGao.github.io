Cite Unseen adds small categorical indicators to citations on Wikipedia. The indicators do not attempt to declare whether a source is trustworthy; they expose useful context that is otherwise hidden behind a numbered link.

The project is an experiment in progressive disclosure: give readers one more piece of evidence while keeping the article itself calm and readable.
