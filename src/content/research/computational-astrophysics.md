My research sits at the intersection of astrophysics, statistics, and software. I am particularly interested in problems where observations are sparse or noisy, models are physically structured, and uncertainty is part of the result.

[`spectral_fit`](https://github.com/QZGao/spectral_fit) is one result of this work: a Python package for fitting and comparing pulsar radio-spectrum models with Bayesian methods.
